MILK SHAKE

  • Ingredients:


    • > 2 cups vanilla ice cream (or your favorite flavor)
    > 1 cup milk (whole or low-fat)
    > 1-2 tablespoons chocolate syrup (optional, for chocolate milkshake)
    > Whipped cream (for topping)
    > Maraschino cherries (for garnish, optional)
    > Chocolate shavings or sprinkles (optional)

  • Instructions:

    • Step 1: Blend the Ingredients


    > In a blender, combine the vanilla ice cream and milk.
    > If making a chocolate milkshake, add the chocolate syrup.
    > Blend until smooth and creamy. Adjust the thickness by adding more milk if needed.

    Step 2: Serve


    > Pour the milkshake into tall glasses.
    > Top with whipped cream, and if desired, garnish with a maraschino cherry and chocolate shavings or sprinkles.
