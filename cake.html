CAKE

  • Ingredients:

    • Dry Ingredients:


    > ¾ cup + 2 tbsp all-purpose flour (112 grams)
    > ¼ cup + 2 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder (34 grams)
    > ½ tsp salt
    > ¾ tsp baking powder
    > ¾ tsp baking soda
    > 1 cup organic cane sugar (200 grams)

    Wet Ingredients:


    > 1 large egg
    > ½ cup full-fat milk
    > ¼ cup oil (4 tbsp) (e.g., virgin coconut oil)
    > 1 to 2 tsp vanilla extract
    > ½ cup boiling water

    Optional Chocolate Buttercream Frosting:


    > ½ cup unsalted butter (soft but cold)
    > 1¾ cup powdered sugar
    > 4 tbsp cocoa powder
    > 1½ tbsp milk or whipping cream
    > 1 tsp vanilla extract

  • Instructions


    • > Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) for at least 15 minutes.
    >  Grease and line two 6-inch cake pans or one 7 or 8-inch pan.
    >  In a mixing bowl, sieve together the flour, cocoa powder, salt, baking powder, and baking soda. Stir in the sugar until well combined.
    >  Make a well in the center of the dry ingredients and add the egg, oil, milk, and vanilla extract. Whisk together until just combined.
    >  Carefully pour in the boiling water and whisk until fully incorporated; the batter will be runny.
    > Pour the batter evenly into the prepared pans. Tap the pans gently on the counter to remove air bubbles. Bake for 28 to 30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
    >  Allow the cakes to cool in their pans for about 10 minutes, then invert onto a wire rack to cool completely before frosting.
