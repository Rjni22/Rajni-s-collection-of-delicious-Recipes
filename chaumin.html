CHAUMIN

  • Ingredients:


    • > 200 grams of noodles (egg or wheat noodles)
    > 2 tablespoons oil (vegetable or sesame oil)
    > 1 medium onion, finely chopped
    > 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, beans, and cabbage), finely chopped
    > 2-3 green chilies, slit (adjust to taste)
    > 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
    > 2-3 tablespoons soy sauce
    > 1 tablespoon vinegar
    > 1 tablespoon chili sauce (optional, for heat)
    > Salt, to taste
    > Pepper, to taste
    > Spring onions, chopped (for garnish)

  • Instructions:

      • Step 1: Cook the Noodles


      > Boil water in a large pot. Add a pinch of salt and cook the noodles according to package instructions until al dente.
      > Drain the noodles and rinse them under cold water to stop cooking. Toss with a little oil to prevent sticking.

      Step 2: Stir-Fry the Vegetables


      > In a large pan or wok, heat the oil over medium heat.
      > Add the chopped onions and sauté until translucent.
      > Add the ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Sauté for a minute until fragrant.
      > Add the mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until they are cooked but still crisp.

      Step 3: Combine Noodles and Sauce


      > Add the cooked noodles to the vegetable mixture.
      > Pour in the soy sauce, vinegar, chili sauce (if using), salt, and pepper. Toss everything together gently to combine.

      Step 4: Serve


      > Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.

    • Garnish with chopped spring onions before serving.
