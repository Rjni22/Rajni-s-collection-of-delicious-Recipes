PANEER SALAD

  • Ingredients:


    • > 200 grams paneer (cottage cheese), cubed
    > 1 cup mixed salad greens (lettuce, spinach, or arugula)
    > 1 medium cucumber, diced
    > 1 medium tomato, diced
    > 1 bell pepper (red or yellow), diced
    > 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
    > 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped (optional)
    > 2 tablespoons olive oil
    > 1 tablespoon lemon juice
    > Salt, to taste
    > Pepper, to taste
    > Fresh coriander or parsley, chopped (for garnish)

  • Instructions:

      • Step 1: Prepare the Vegetables


      > Wash and chop all the vegetables: cucumber, tomato, bell pepper, and onion.
      > In a large bowl, combine the salad greens, cucumber, tomato, bell pepper, onion, and green chilies (if using).

      Step 2: Add Paneer


      > Add the cubed paneer to the bowl of vegetables.

      Step 3: Make the Dressing


      > In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.

      Step 4: Combine Salad and Dressing


      > Drizzle the dressing over the salad and gently toss everything together to combine.

      Step 5: Serve


      > Garnish with fresh coriander or parsley.
      > Serve immediately as a light meal or side dish.
