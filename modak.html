MODAK

Ingredients

    Ingredients for the Dough (Outer Layer):


    > 1 cup rice flour
    > 1 cup water
    > 1 teaspoon ghee (clarified butter)
    > A pinch of salt

    Ingredients for the Filling:


    > 1 cup grated fresh coconut
    > 3/4 cup grated jaggery
    > 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
    > 1 teaspoon poppy seeds (optional)
    > 1 tablespoon chopped nuts, like cashews or almonds (optional)

    h1

  • Instructions:

      • Step 1: Prepare the Filling


      > In a pan, add the grated coconut and jaggery. Cook on low heat, stirring continuously until the jaggery melts and combines with the coconut.
      > Add cardamom powder, poppy seeds, and chopped nuts (if using).
      > Cook until the mixture thickens slightly, then set it aside to cool.

      Step 2: Prepare the Dough


      > In a separate pan, bring the water to a boil. Add a pinch of salt and 1 teaspoon of ghee.
      > Lower the heat and gradually add the rice flour while stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
      > Stir until the dough comes together and forms a smooth, soft ball. Remove from heat, cover, and let it cool slightly.

      Step 3: Shape the Modak


      > Knead the dough lightly to make it smooth. Divide it into small, equal portions.
      > Take one portion and flatten it in your palms to form a small disc.
      > Place a spoonful of filling in the center. Gently press and shape the edges into pleats, then bring them together at the top to form a peak, sealing the Modak.

      Step 4: Steam the Modak


      > Grease a steamer plate with ghee and place the Modaks on it.
      > Steam the Modaks for 10-15 minutes on medium heat until they become slightly translucent.
      > Remove from the steamer and let them cool slightly.
