FRIED RICE

  • Ingredients:


    • > 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old rice)
    > 2 tablespoons oil (vegetable or sesame oil)
    > 1 medium onion, chopped
    > 2-3 garlic cloves, minced
    > 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, bell peppers, corn, etc.)
    > 2-3 green onions, chopped
    > 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    > 1 tablespoon oyster sauce (optional)
    > 1 teaspoon sesame oil (optional)
    > Salt, to taste
    > Pepper, to taste
    > 2 eggs (optional, for added protein)
    > Cooked chicken, shrimp, or tofu (optional, for protein)

  • Instructions:

    • Step 1: Prepare the Rice


    > If using freshly cooked rice, spread it out on a tray to cool and dry slightly. Day-old rice works best as it’s firmer and less sticky.

    Step 2: Heat the Oil


    > In a large pan or wok, heat the oil over medium-high heat.

    Step 3: Sauté Aromatics


    > Add the chopped onion and garlic to the pan. Sauté until the onions are translucent and fragrant.

    Step 4: Cook Vegetables


    > Add the mixed vegetables to the pan and stir-fry for about 3-4 minutes until they are tender but still crisp.

    Step 5: Scramble Eggs (Optional)


    > If using eggs, push the vegetables to one side of the pan, crack the eggs into the other side, and scramble until fully cooked. Then mix with the vegetables.

    Step 6: Add Rice and Sauce


    > Add the cooked rice to the pan, breaking up any clumps. Stir well to combine with the vegetables and eggs.
    > Pour in the soy sauce, oyster sauce (if using), and sesame oil (if using). Stir everything together and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.

    Step 7: Season and Serve


    > Taste and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper as needed.
    > Add chopped green onions before serving.
