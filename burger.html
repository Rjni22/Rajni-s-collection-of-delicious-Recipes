Burger

  • Ingredients:


    • > 3 medium potatoes (boiled and mashed)
    > 100 g carrots (grated)
    > 40 g French beans (chopped)
    > ½ cup green peas (fresh or frozen)
    > 1 medium onion (finely chopped)
    > 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste

    Spices:


    > ½ tsp coriander powder
    > ½ tsp cumin powder
    > ½ tsp garam masala
    > ½ tsp red chili powder
    > A pinch of turmeric powder
    > Salt to taste
    > Bread crumbs as needed for binding
    > Whole wheat burger buns

  • Instructions:

    • Prepare the Patty Mixture:


    > In a pan, heat oil and sauté onions until translucent.
    >  Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until fragrant.
    >  Combine with mashed potatoes, grated carrots, chopped beans, peas, and spices.
    >  Mix well and add bread crumbs to bind. Shape the Patties:
    > Form the mixture into burger-sized patties. Cook the Patties:
    > Shallow fry or bake the patties until golden brown on both sides. Assemble the Burgers:
    >  Serve on whole wheat buns with your choice of toppings such as lettuce, tomato slices, cucumber slices, and sauces.
    These recipes offer a great way to enjoy homemade burgers tailored to your taste preferences!
