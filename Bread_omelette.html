BREAD OMELETTE

  • Ingredients:


    • > 2 large eggs
    > 2 slices of bread
    > 1 small onion, finely chopped
    > 1 small tomato, finely chopped
    > 1 green chili, finely chopped (optional)
    > 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro (coriander)
    > Salt, to taste
    > Black pepper, to taste
    > 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder (optional)
    > 1/4 teaspoon red chili powder (optional)
    > 1 tablespoon butter or oil (for frying)

  • Instructions:

    • Step 1: Prepare the Egg Mixture


    > In a bowl, crack the eggs and whisk them well.
    > Add the chopped onion, tomato, green chili, cilantro, salt, black pepper, turmeric powder, and red chili powder. Mix everything thoroughly.

    Step 2: Cook the Bread Omelette


    > Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
    > Pour half of the egg mixture into the pan, spreading it out evenly.
    > Place a slice of bread on top of the egg mixture, pressing down lightly so it sticks to the egg.
    > After about 1 minute, flip the bread and egg over, letting the other side cook. Place another slice of bread on top of the flipped side to make a sandwich.

    Step 3: Finish and Serve


    > Cook for another 1-2 minutes until both sides are golden brown and the egg is cooked through.
    > Remove from the pan and repeat with the remaining egg mixture and bread slices.

    Serve hot with ketchup, chutney, or any dipping sauce of your choice.
