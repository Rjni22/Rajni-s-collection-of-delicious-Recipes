FRUIT SALAD

  • Ingredients:


    • > 2 apples, diced
    > 2 bananas, sliced
    > 1 cup grapes, halved
    > 1 cup strawberries, sliced
    > 1 cup pineapple chunks (fresh or canned)
    > 1 orange, segmented
    > 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
    > 1 tablespoon lemon juice
    > Fresh mint leaves (for garnish)

  • Instructions:

      • Step 1: Prepare the Fruits


      > Wash all the fruits thoroughly.
      > Dice the apples, slice the bananas, halve the grapes, slice the strawberries, and cut the pineapple into chunks.
      > Segment the orange and set aside.

      Step 2: Mix the Fruits


      > In a large mixing bowl, combine all the prepared fruits: apples, bananas, grapes, strawberries, pineapple, and orange.

      Step 3: Add Dressing


      > Drizzle honey (if using) and lemon juice over the mixed fruits.
      > Gently toss the salad to coat the fruits evenly with the dressing.

      Step 4: Serve

      Garnish with fresh mint leaves. Serve immediately or chill in the refrigerator for about 30 minutes before serving.
