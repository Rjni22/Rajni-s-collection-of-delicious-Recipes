PIZZA

Ingredients

    Ingredients for the Dough:


    > 2 ¼ teaspoons (1 packet) active dry yeast
    > 1 ½ cups warm water (110°F/45°C)
    > 3 ½ - 4 cups all-purpose flour
    > 2 tablespoons olive oil
    > 2 teaspoons salt
    > 1 teaspoon sugar

    Ingredients for the Sauce:


    > 1 can (15 oz) crushed tomatoes
    > 1 clove garlic, minced
    > 1 tablespoon olive oil
    > 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    > Salt and pepper, to taste

    Ingredients for the Toppings:


    > 1 ½ - 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
    > Fresh basil leaves (optional)
    > Olive oil, for brushing the crust
    > Additional toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, olives, etc.)

    Instructions:

    Step 1: Prepare the Dough


    > In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast and sugar in warm water. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until frothy.
    > In a large mixing bowl, combine 3 ½ cups of flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil.
    > Mix until the dough starts to come together. Add more flour if needed, 1 tablespoon at a time, until the dough is smooth and elastic but not sticky.
    > Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 8-10 minutes.
    > Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a cloth, and let it rise for 1-1.5 hours until doubled in size.

    Step 2: Prepare the Sauce


    > In a small saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
    > Add the crushed tomatoes, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens slightly.
    > Set aside to cool.

    Step 3: Preheat the Oven


    > Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C) and, if using a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat.

    Step 4: Assemble the Pizza


    > Punch down the dough and divide it into two equal portions for two medium pizzas or one large pizza.
    > Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
    > Place the dough on a baking sheet or pizza stone. Spread a layer of sauce on the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
    > Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the sauce and add your desired toppings.

    Step 5: Bake the Pizza


    > Brush the crust with olive oil for a golden finish.
    > Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the crust is golden brown.
    > Remove from the oven, let cool slightly, and garnish with fresh basil if desired.
