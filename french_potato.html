FRENCH POTATOES

  • Ingredients:


    • > 4 large potatoes (Russet potatoes work best)
    > Cold water (for soaking)
    > Salt, to taste
    > Vegetable oil, for frying

  • Instructions:

    • Step 1: Prepare the Potatoes


    > Peel the potatoes (optional) and cut them into evenly sized sticks, about 1/4 to 1/2 inch thick.
    > Place the cut potatoes in a bowl of cold water and soak for at least 30 minutes to remove excess starch. For best results, soak for 1-2 hours.

    Step 2: Par-Cook the Fries


    > Drain the potatoes and pat them dry with paper towels or a kitchen towel.
    > Heat oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 300째F (150째C).
    > Fry the potatoes in batches for about 4-5 minutes. They should be softened but not golden yet. Remove and drain on paper towels.

    Step 3: Final Fry for Crispiness


    > Increase the oil temperature to 375째F (190째C).
    > Fry the potatoes again in batches for about 2-3 minutes until golden brown and crispy.
    > Remove and drain on fresh paper towels. Sprinkle with salt while still hot.
